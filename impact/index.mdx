## Funding

Support for this project came (in part) from an Emory Public Health Bioinformatics Fellowship
funded by the [CDC Emerging Infections Program](https://dph.georgia.gov/EIP), the
[Wyoming Public Health Division](https://health.wyo.gov/publichealth/), the
[Center for Applied Pathogen Epidemiology and Outbreak Control (CAPE)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/center-for-applied-pathogen-epidemiology-and-outbreak-control/),
and the [CZI Open Science Program (EOSS6)](https://chanzuckerberg.com/eoss/proposals/enhancing-the-bactopia-ecosystem-with-trainings-and-visual-reports/).
